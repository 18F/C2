Communicart Cartmaker bookmarklet

You can now assemble carts from any site and send them for approval. Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Drag the link below to the bookmark toolbar

    Here's the link to the cartmaker.

  2. Navigate to the item you want to buy online

  3. Click the bookmarklet.

    In most cases, the bookmarklet will automatically figure out the price of the item. In some cases you'll need to enter it manually.

  4. Click Add to Cart to add the item to your cart.

  5. When you've added all the items you wish to buy, enter the approval group name and click Send for Approval.
